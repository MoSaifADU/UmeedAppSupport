Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 16, 2024
Last Updated: December 16, 2024

Welcome to Umeed. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Umeed is a community services platform that connects residents in rural areas of Kashmir and Pakistan with essential local services including tutors, taxi services, laundry, air conditioning repair, plumbing, and other household services.

Important: By using the Umeed app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register and use Umeed, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name: To identify you within the app and facilitate service connections
  • Phone Number: To enable communication between service providers and users, and for account verification

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our app, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features accessed, time spent on app, service requests made
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, app version

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

2.1 To Provide Services

  • Connect you with local service providers (tutors, taxi drivers, plumbers, etc.)
  • Facilitate communication between users and service providers
  • Process and manage service requests
  • Verify user accounts and prevent fraud

2.2 To Improve Our App

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Develop new features and services
  • Fix technical issues and bugs
  • Understand which services are most needed in different areas

2.3 To Communicate With You

  • Send service-related notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Inform you about important updates or changes to our services

3. How We Share Your Information

We respect your privacy and only share your information in the following limited circumstances:

3.1 With Service Providers

When you request a service (tutor, taxi, plumber, etc.), we share your name and phone number with the relevant service provider so they can fulfill your request and contact you directly.

3.2 Service Providers to Our App

We may share information with third-party service providers who help us operate our app, such as:

  • Cloud hosting services
  • Analytics services (to understand app usage)
  • Communication services (for notifications)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the services they provide to us.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Umeed, our users, or the public.

3.4 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure servers and databases
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal information (only authorized personnel)

However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or legitimate business purposes.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your name and phone number at any time through the app settings.

6.2 Delete Your Account

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated personal data. To request account deletion, please visit: Account Deletion Page

6.3 Withdraw Consent

If you no longer wish to use our services, you can stop using the app and request account deletion at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

Umeed is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you through the app or via email if the changes are significant
  • Post the updated policy on this page

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your country, where data protection laws may differ. By using Umeed, you consent to the transfer of your information to Pakistan and other countries where we operate.

10. Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any information to them.

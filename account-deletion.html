Account Deletion Request

UMEED Foundation

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and associated data from our app, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to: info@umeed.org.pk
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include in your email:
    • Your full name (as registered in the app)
    • Phone number (if provided during registration)
  4. We will process your request within 30 days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Profile data and settings
  • Donation history and transaction records
  • User preferences and app activity logs
  • Any uploaded documents or images
  • Account credentials and authentication data

Important: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, you will not be able to recover your account or data.

Contact Information

Email: info@umeed.org.pk

Response Time: We typically respond within 2-3 business days

Deletion Timeline: Account and data deletion will be completed within 30 days of your request

Questions?

If you have any questions about our data deletion process or privacy practices, please contact us at the email address above or refer to our Privacy Policy.
